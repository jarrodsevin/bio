Career Milestones

At Northern Tool + Equipment, I was honored with both Store Manager of the Year and Store of the Year distinctions — selected from a pool of over 120 locations nationwide. These awards were based on performance in key retail metrics including top-line revenue, year-over-year sales comps, warranty penetration, shrink control, and internal audit excellence.

One of my proudest accomplishments was earning a Top 3 performance score during an intensive internal audit conducted by corporate leadership at Northern Tool + Equipment. These audits measured safety compliance, operational consistency, and executional discipline — areas in which I’ve maintained industry-leading standards across multiple years.

I was also entrusted to relocate to Florida to help launch a brand-new division for Northern Tool + Equipment — the Field Service Team. This initiative was built from the ground up and involved hiring and developing a team of remote field service professionals, creating structured workflows, and implementing efficient quoting systems. I led business development efforts with corporate clients, developed pricing strategies, and oversaw multi-phase, high-value service projects from concept through completion. These initiatives often spanned months and required hands-on leadership, coordination, and long-term operational oversight.

As Regional Service Manager, I was responsible for the sales and operations of 22 service centers across 7 states. I held full P&L accountability, developed sales strategies, optimized warranty performance, maintained high operational standards, and personally handled high-priority customer escalations. This role demanded strong regional coordination and consistency in service excellence across all locations.

Recognitions

At Northern Tool + Equipment, I was consistently recognized for leadership, execution, and talent development. As a Regional Training Manager, I played a key role in onboarding and mentoring newly promoted and externally hired store managers. These leaders would begin their structured development in my store, where I worked closely with them on operations, leadership presence, and cultural alignment. I later joined them in their home locations for in-market coaching, giving them personalized follow-through and support as they took ownership of their stores.

This program not only helped shape the next generation of field leaders, it also shaped me — sharpening my ability to scale mentorship and tailor my coaching to various leadership styles. It was one of the most fulfilling responsibilities of my career.

Additionally, I was frequently selected to serve as the Regional New Store Opening Manager for Northern Tool + Equipment, where I became the go-to expert for launching new locations. I mentored brand-new store managers and teams—most of whom were entering the company for the first time. My leadership spanned every phase of store readiness: merchandising, inventory control, visual setups, planogram execution, team onboarding, sales training, and operational processes. I was trusted to be the one who brought order, energy, and culture to a new location from day one.
